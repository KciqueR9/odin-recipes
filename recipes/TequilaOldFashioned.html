Tequila Old Fashioned

Description

Traditionally, an old fashioned is made with whiskey or bourbon (or even brandy if you're from Wisconsin), but smooth a√±ejo tequila sets this recipe apart from all the rest. Tequila is distilled from agave plants, so using an agave syrup is the perfect choice to give a touch of sweetness to this drink.

Ingredients

  • 4 maraschino cherries, stems removed, divided
  • 1 orange slice
  • 6 ice cubes, or more if desired
  • 2 fluid ounces anejo tequila
  • 1 fluid ounce Cointreau
  • 3 dashes bitters
  • 1 orange twist

Steps

  1. Place orange slice and 2 cherries in a rocks glass, and crush with a muddler.

  2. Add ice cubes to glass

  3. Add tequila, Cointreau and bitters; stir.

  4. Garnish with additional 2 cherries and an orange twist.
